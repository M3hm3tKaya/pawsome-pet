# Pawsome Pet Hotel - Project Brief

## Overview
Pawsome Pet Hotel is a 6-page website for a pet hotel located in Cekmekoy, Istanbul. The site features a fun, warm, and colorful design aimed at pet owners seeking premium care services for their pets.

## Target Audience
Pet owners in Istanbul looking for reliable pet boarding, daycare, spa, and training services.

## Core Pages
1. Home (Ana Sayfa)
2. Services (Hizmetler)
3. Rooms & Pricing (Odalar)
4. Gallery (Galeri)
5. About Us (Hakkimizda)
6. Contact & Reservation (Iletisim)

## Design Direction
- Fun, warm, colorful aesthetic
- Rounded corners and pill-shaped buttons
- Bounce animations and paw print decorations
- Pet-friendly, inviting atmosphere

## Business Goals
- Drive reservations through the contact form
- Showcase services and room options
- Build trust with testimonials and team profiles
- Differentiate with live camera and VIP services
